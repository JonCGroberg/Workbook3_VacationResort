Unwind at Sea Cliff Resort.

A Coastal Oasis

Nestled along 100 acres of coastal headlands, Sea Cliff Resort offers the perfect retreat from everyday life. Wake to the sounds of crashing waves as you take in the fresh sea air.

Luxury Meets Nature

Modern yet relaxed, our rooms and suites provide comfortable accommodation with ocean or mountain views. Spend lazy days lounging poolside or take advantage of our full-service spa.

Farm-Fresh Dining

Dine al fresco at Tide's Edge restaurant, featuring the freshest local seafood and farm-to-table ingredients. After sunset, cozy cabanas at the beach bar are the ideal spot to unwind with live music and drinks
